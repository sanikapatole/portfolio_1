Recipe Instructions

INGREDIENTS

    Pasta:
  • Fettuccine or any pasta of your choice
      • For the Alfredo Sauce:
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream (or whipping cream)
  • Garlic, minced or finely chopped
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Optionally, nutmeg or Italian seasoning for flavor
      • Optional Additions:
  • Cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • Cooked shrimp
  • Broccoli florets
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Spinach leaves
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Peas
  • Bacon, cooked and crumbled

    • INSTRUCTIONS

    To make Alfredo Pasta, cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. In a separate pan, melt butter and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant. Pour in heavy cream and simmer until slightly thickened. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Toss cooked pasta in the Alfredo sauce until well coated. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley if desired. Enjoy your creamy and comforting Alfredo Pasta!
